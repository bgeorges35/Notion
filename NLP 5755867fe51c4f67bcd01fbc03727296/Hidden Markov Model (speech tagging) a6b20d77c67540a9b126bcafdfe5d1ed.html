Hidden Markov Model

or HMM

A Markov chain where the states are "hidden" and can't be observed . There's an other process that depend on the Markov chain that we observe to learn about the hidden markov chain.

  • Emission probability → Probability of an observable when we are in a particulat hidden state
  • Emission Matrix → Matrix of the emission probability (like the transition matrix). The raw sum (prob for one hidden state to be all his observable) is always one BUT the column sum is not (probability for all hidden state to be one observable)

Here: the tags are hidden state and the words are observable
